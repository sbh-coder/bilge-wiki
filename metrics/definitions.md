# Metric Definitions

## CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)

**Definition:** Total ad spend divided by new bookings/customers acquired. Tracked per channel and vertical.
**Current benchmarks (Apr 2026):**
- HC iOS Meta: $72
- IV therapy: $70 (improved from $90)
- Deep cleaning: $365 (spiked; baseline $150-200)
- Pest control: $51 (improved)
- Laundry: $84 (over budget)
- Abu Dhabi HC: $35 | Dubai HC: $83
- TikTok app re-engagement: $6
- TikTok Android re-engagement: $14

## Monthly Revenue Target

**Definition:** Total GMV/revenue from paid channels per month.
**Current benchmark:** $650K/month HC target. MTD Apr 14: $273K, tracking $625-630K.

## Impression Share

**Definition:** Percentage of eligible impressions your ads actually received.
**Current benchmark:** STD test: 43% (competition rising — budget increase triggered).

## Frequency

**Definition:** Average number of times a unique user sees an ad. Saturation threshold: 3.5.
**Current benchmark:** Arab ad sets at 19x and 28x — heavily saturated.

## Search Pressure

**Definition:** Internal metric for how competitive the keyword auction is for a given campaign. High = more spend needed for same visibility.
**Current benchmark:** Deep cleaning: 90% (critical). AC: 73%.

## Budget Pacing

**Definition:** Actual spend vs. planned spend at a given point in the month/day.
**Current benchmark:** Laundry spending 30% over budget (needs CPC reduction).

## WA (WhatsApp) Campaign Cost

**Definition:** Monthly spend on WhatsApp campaigns across all verticals.
**Current benchmark:** ~$21K/month. Under review for correct vertical attribution in Power BI.

## PMAX Spend

**Definition:** Performance Max campaign daily budget on Google.
**Current benchmark:** $700/day with good metrics.
